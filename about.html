Welcome to Your Smile's New Home: Dent'im Dental Clinic



About Us

At Dent'im, your smile is our passion. Established in 2025, we are a modern dental clinic located in the heart of Tirana, dedicated to providing high-quality dental care in a friendly and comfortable environment. Our goal is to make every visit a positive experience for our patients.

We believe in using the latest dental technology and techniques to provide the best possible care. Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that our patients receive top-notch treatment. We also prioritize patient education, helping you understand your oral health and the treatments we provide.

Our Approach

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services, including preventive care, restorative treatments, cosmetic dentistry, and emergency dental care. Whether you need a routine check-up, teeth whitening, or a more complex procedure, we have got you covered.

Our team of highly skilled and experienced dentists, hygienists, and support staff are committed to ensuring your oral health and well-being. We take pride in offering personalized care tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient.

Our Team

Andi Molla
Dental Hygienist
