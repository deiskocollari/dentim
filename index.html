At Dent'im Clinic, we are committed to provide the best care for your oral health. Our team has years of experience and is trained to offer you services of the highest level. We pay attention to every detail and offer personalized treatments for each patient.

Our Services:

  • General Dentistry: Routine check-ups, cleanings, preventive care, fluoride treatments, sealants.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Teeth whitening, veneers, smile makeovers, bonding.
  • Orthodontics: Traditional braces, Invisalign, clear aligners, retainers.
  • Restorative Dentistry: Fillings, crowns, bridges, dental implants, dentures, root canals.
  • Pediatric Dentistry: Child-friendly care, preventive treatments, habit counseling.
  • Oral Surgery: Extractions, wisdom teeth removal, bone grafts, sinus lifts.
  • Periodontics: Gum disease treatment, scaling, root planing, soft tissue grafting.
  • Dental Tourism Services: Personalized plans, travel coordination, transportation arrangements, accommodation coordination.
